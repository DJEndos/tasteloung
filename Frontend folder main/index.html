Our Story

About Taste Lounge

👩‍🍳
Chef B. (Blessing Kalu)
Founder & Executive Chef · Since 2025

Taste Lounge was born in 2025 from a dream — to bring the rich, diverse and deeply soulful flavours of Nigerian cuisine to every doorstep. Founded by Chef Blessing Kalu, who grew up watching her grandmother transform simple ingredients into unforgettable meals.

Every dish we serve is a tribute to Nigeria's vibrant food culture — from the smoky Jollof rice of the Southwest to the hearty pepper soups of the South-South, the spiced Suya of the North and the rich Egusi soups of the East. We cook with love, serve with pride.

100% fresh ingredients sourced locally from Nigerian markets daily
No artificial preservatives — every meal made from scratch
Quick ordering via WhatsApp and Email for your convenience
Catering services available for events, parties & celebrations
